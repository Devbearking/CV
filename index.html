Why did i decited to become a Programmer

In a world full of IT why did I decide to become one and why now? Well, the simple answer to this question is, You guessed it "MONEY". But money is not everything, there are much more important things in life like love and friends. ... A few years ago I went to a course for web development with HTML and CSS.This one 12-hour lecture was enough for me to lose any interest in it, but I was only 16 and was more distracted back then, so I didn't think about it much. Fast forward to 2020 when I was working in a warehouse in The Netherlands I had a colleague who was making changes to the software we worked with, and man... was I impressed, I had some relatively basic knowledge about what he did and he inspired me to start learning more about it. So I did, chose my first course at "Udemy" and began the journey. A few mounts later I took his position and became a Key User.

My journey so far.

After deciding to go back to Bulgaria, I fully dedicated myself to my education and successfully graduated the first stage of "Software University" "Programming basics with python". Now after I got a excellent result on my exam, I was accepted for the next stage "Programming fundamentals" starting in September.

My goal!

My overall goal is to become a full stack developer, I'm still far from there and for now, I'm looking for opportunities to jumpstart my career. I will continue to educate myself until i reach my goal.
